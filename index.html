About Us

For more than 12 years, Harisar Construction has been a trusted name across the Lower Mainland and Vancouver area. We specialize in delivering high-quality landscape construction, concrete solutions, and architectural concrete forming tailored to residential, commercial, and municipal projects.

Our success is built on craftsmanship, reliability, and a commitment to bringing our clients' visions to life. Whether we're shaping outdoor spaces, constructing durable concrete structures, or creating custom architectural finishes, we strive for excellence in every detail.
