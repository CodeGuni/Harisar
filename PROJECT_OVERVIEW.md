# Harisar Constructions Ltd - Website Overview

## Project Summary
A modern, fully responsive website for Harisar Constructions Ltd showcasing your construction projects and company information.

## What's Included

1. **Home/Hero Section** - Eye-catching introduction with company branding
2. **About Section** - Company information with key statistics
3. **Projects Section** - Showcase of 5 featured projects:

   - Terrayne
   - Baden Park
   - The Commons Langley

4. **Contact Section** - Contact information with clickable email and Google Maps location
